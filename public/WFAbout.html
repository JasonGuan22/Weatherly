About Weatherly

Weatherly is a modern weather and air quality dashboard designed to provide real-time updates and insights. Our mission is to empower individuals with accurate weather forecasts and air pollution data to improve daily decision-making and promote public safety.

What to Expect

With Weatherly, you can access:

  • Detailed weather forecasts for your location and beyond.
  • Real-time air quality updates to help you stay informed about environmental conditions.
  • A user-friendly interface with modern design for seamless navigation.

The Information Problem We Address

Weatherly was created to tackle the challenge of making weather and air quality data more accessible and actionable. Many individuals struggle to find reliable, real-time environmental information in a format that is easy to understand. This lack of accessible data can lead to missed opportunities for preparation, especially in the face of severe weather or poor air quality conditions.

By providing a centralized platform that integrates weather and pollution data, we aim to close this gap, helping people make informed choices about their daily activities and long-term planning.
